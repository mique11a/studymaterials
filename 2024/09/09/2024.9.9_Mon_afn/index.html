电荷与库仑定律

电的发展历程:

电荷

charge is quantized(电荷是量子化的) 1e ≈ 1.610^-19
Neutron(中子) -> n
Proton(质子) -> p
Quark() -> q
1p = 3q
1n = 3q
q has 2 kinds of charge: -1/3e and 2/3e
$2 2/3 + 1*(-1/3) = +1$
$1* 2/3 + 2*(-1/3) = 0$
应用：

  • 静电喷漆（paint spray）
  • 复印(Photocopying)
